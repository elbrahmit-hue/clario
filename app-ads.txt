To get Google AdMob to successfully crawl your `app-ads.txt` file from GitHub Pages, follow these exact steps to set up your repository and paste the correct link into App Store Connect.

### Step 1: Set up the files on GitHub

For AdMob to find the file, your GitHub Pages repository must serve it at the absolute root domain.

1. Go to your main GitHub Pages repository (`etabra098.github.io`).
2. Create a new file named exactly **`app-ads.txt`** (make sure it's lowercase and uses `app-ads`, not just `ads`).
3. Paste your AdMob line inside it:
```text

```



google.com, pub-1682551170494568, DIRECT, f08c47fec0942fa0

```
4. Make sure your empty `index.html` file is also in this same root folder so GitHub Pages builds properly.

### Step 2: Test your links in a browser
Before heading to App Store Connect, open a new browser tab and test these two URLs to ensure they load perfectly:
* **`[https://etabra098.github.io/](https://etabra098.github.io/)`** (Should show your empty/blank page)
* **`[https://etabra098.github.io/app-ads.txt](https://etabra098.github.io/app-ads.txt)`** (Should display your text line)

---

### Step 3: Paste the link into App Store Connect

Google AdMob automatically strips subfolders and paths when it crawls your site. It looks directly at the root domain listed in your store presence.

1. Log into **App Store Connect**.
2. Go to **Apps** and select your application.
3. On the left sidebar, click on your current app version under **App Store** (e.g., *1.0.0 Prepare for Submission*).
4. Scroll down to the **App Information** or **Contact Information** section.
5. In the **Marketing URL** field, paste your root domain link:
   > `[https://etabra098.github.io/](https://etabra098.github.io/)`
6. *(Optional)* It is highly recommended to also paste `[https://etabra098.github.io/](https://etabra098.github.io/)` into the **Support URL** field if you don't have a separate support page yet, as Apple requires this field to be filled out.
7. Click **Save** in the top right corner.

Once your app update is submitted and approved, Google AdMob's crawler will visit `[https://etabra098.github.io/](https://etabra098.github.io/)`, automatically append `/app-ads.txt` to it, and verify your publisher status. It usually takes 24 hours for the status to turn green in your AdMob dashboard after the crawler runs.
```
